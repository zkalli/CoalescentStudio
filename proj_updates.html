10/8/2019: Game Concept Art

Our team has brainstormed two game ideas, both with a focus on cooperative play. To help convey those ideas and their look, Kallista has put together some concept art for what we are currently calling War Smith and Soul Link. War Smith is a horde based tower defense game, while Soul Link is a 3D side-scrolling platformer. Stay tuned for more details!
