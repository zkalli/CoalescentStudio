12/4/2019: ‘Twas the Night Before Capstone

With our final presentation of the semester coming up tomorrow afternoon, we are happy to share our complete final presentation here . It reflects the direction we will be going come January 2020, when we will get into the full swing of development. The information in the presentation is based on our findings from our Requirements Gathering and Project Charter. You can also keep up with the prototype development we have completed on our YouTube Channel to find some short animations for the warrior and programming tests. And if you happen to enjoy Twitter, you can also follow our updates there too! @CoalescentStud1


11/21/2019: Game Pitches

We’ve been busy these past weeks defining all the details of our project, War Smith, in our project charter. We’ve consulted with our sponsors and advisor and were glad to be able to maintain creative control of the project. Along with the charter, we’ve put together our game design document and a Gantt chart to outline the schedule all the way to the end. The game will be complete in early April 2020 and released on Steam later that month!

    • 10/8/2019: Game Concept Art

    Our team has brainstormed two game ideas, both with a focus on cooperative play. To help convey those ideas and their look, Kallista has put together some concept art for what we are currently calling War Smith and Soul Link. War Smith is a horde based tower defense game, while Soul Link is a 3D side-scrolling platformer. Stay tuned for more details!
